The Problem

Precision manufacturing is broken

Regional Job Shops

4–8 week lead times and growing backlogs. Shops turning away new work due to maxed capacity. Can't provide digital traceability or modern quality documentation customers now require. Owners eyeing retirement with no clear succession plans or exit strategies.

Online Marketplaces

Routing to the same overloaded regional shops or questionable foreign facilities. Quality variances. No true traceability. Premium pricing with marketplace markup and fees stacked on shop costs.

Overseas Manufacturing

3–5 week ocean freight or 3-5× air shipping costs. Time zone friction. IP vulnerability. Customs delays and unpredictability. Tariff exposure and uncertainty. Forced minimum.

In-House Shops

Requires major capex for limited utilization. Significant challenges finding and/or keeping skilled machinists. Doesn't scale with demand.

Our Solution

Integrated manufacturing infrastructure that combines advanced machining, robotics, and process intelligence to deliver precision parts at scale in 48 hours, not 4-8 weeks

Advanced manufacturing equipment is readily available: 5-axis CNC machines, industrial robotics, precision metrology systems. The problem isn't the hardware - it's that job shops operate each machine as an independent workstation requiring constant human intervention. Manual setups, isolated quality checks, and sequential workflows create constant stops and starts. The result: 4-8 week bottlenecks and inconsistent quality.

We're building coordinated manufacturing systems where equipment operates in concert as integrated cells, not isolated machines. Upload a design file, and automated process planning orchestrates the entire workflow: toolpath generation, fixture selection, and operation scheduling across machining centers. Robotic systems move parts seamlessly through machining, inspection, and finishing. Closed-loop control adjusts cutting parameters in real-time based on sensor feedback. Every machine, robot, and sensor synchronized for continuous lights-out production. Complete digital traceability from raw stock to certified delivery. Precision parts in 48 hours, not 4-8 weeks.
